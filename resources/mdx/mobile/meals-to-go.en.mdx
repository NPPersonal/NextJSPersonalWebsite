### Introduction

---

This app help user to find restaurants by location. User is
able to look into restaurant's information and add it to their
favourites.

In addition user can place order and pay for their order
through online payment.

<br />

### Motivation & Purpose

---

The purpose of make this app is to understand how ReactNative
framework work.
