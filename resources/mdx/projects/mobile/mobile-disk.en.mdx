### Introduction

---

An app for IOS platform which turn your iPhone into a disk to store
any files you want. Moreover it allow you to transfer files between
computer and iPhone through cable or Wi-Fi.

<br />

### Motivation & Purpose

---

Since mobile device had became more popular and personal accessory
such as iPhone, turning moible device into file storage disk and carry
it anywhere you go. **Take it wherever you go**. It can be a convenient
option.

<br />

### More

---

The IOS app provide some features. The primary feature is to storage
any files in your iPhone device. You can transfer any type of files
from your computer to your iPhone. On the contrary, you are able to
transfer files from iPhone to your computer either by cable or Wi-Fi.

Aside from primary feature. You can preview files on your iPhone
directly as long as it is supported by the app for preview. For example
you can transfer image or video file to your iPhone and open it on your
iPhone.
